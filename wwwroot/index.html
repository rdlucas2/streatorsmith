Streator Smith 2021 Competition Video Submission Form

Checklist

  • To ensure fairness, we ask that you not identify yourself by name in the video or the video file name. There is no need to address the judge.
  • Only submit ONE VIDEO with BOTH PIECES played back to back. This is the closest experience to an in-person audition.
  • Please wear appropriate performance attire and shoes for the video.
  • Make sure that the performer is in the shot while filming. The judge would like to get a look at your technique, so make sure to capture your hands and head!
  • The name of the file does not matter, but as stated above do not include your name. The default name generated by your recording device, or a format such as "contestant#.ext" - for example, if you are contestant 2, "contestant2.mov" - will do.
  • Video is one of these formats: ".MP4", ".WEBM", ".MPG", ".MP2", ".MPEG", ".MPE", ".MPV", ".OGG", ".M4P", ".M4V", ".AVI", ".WMV", ".MOV", ".QT", ".FLV", ".SWF", "AVCHD".
  • Video is less than 1.5GB in file size. You may need to adjust the quality settings on your recording device to meet this requirement.
  • The code in the form below is written on the audition letter you received in the mail.

Once you complete the form, press submit and wait until you see the success or failure message before leaving the page.
Please have your submission in by 11:59pm on Thursday April 8th.
